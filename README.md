### 🔧 What I'm Building

A validation-grade market and credit risk engine in Python, structured around institutional model governance practices (SR 11-7 / OSFI E-23).

**Core focus areas:**
- Value-at-Risk & Expected Shortfall (Historical, Parametric, Monte Carlo, FHS)
- GARCH(1,1) volatility modelling with MLE parameter estimation
- Backtesting: Kupiec POF + Christoffersen independence + joint conditional coverage
- Credit Expected Loss framework (PD × LGD × EAD)
- Stress testing & scenario analysis
